About DeepFake Audio Detector

We're on a mission to protect authenticity in the digital age through cutting-edge deepfake detection technology.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, DeepFake Audio Detector (DFA) emerged as a response to the growing threat of synthetic media. Our team of AI researchers, security experts, and audio engineers came together with a shared concern: as voice synthesis technology became more accessible and realistic, the potential for misuse grew exponentially.

What began as a research project at MIT's Media Lab quickly evolved into a comprehensive solution for authenticating digital audio. We recognized that traditional methods of verification were becoming obsolete in the face of increasingly sophisticated deepfake technology.

Through collaborative research and development, we pioneered a multi-layered approach to audio verification that combines acoustic forensics, machine learning, and biometric voice analysis to detect even the most convincing synthetic audio.

Today, DFA serves individuals, media organizations, legal institutions, and enterprises worldwide, providing tools to verify audio authenticity with confidence. As technology evolves, so do we—constantly refining our algorithms to stay ahead of emerging deepfake techniques.

Our Mission & Vision
